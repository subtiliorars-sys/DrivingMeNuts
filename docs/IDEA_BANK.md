## IB-001 · "Gusto" — wind-themed character (owner, 2026-06-07)
**Core:** a character named **Gusto** — defining trait: he's *gusty*. Candidate forms
(owner's riffs, all open):
- a **windbag** — talks endlessly, huffs and puffs (comic relief / long-winded regular
  who delays the queue — could literally slow service while he monologues: a pacing
  mechanic with a personality)
- a man who **literally creates gusts** — huffing/puffing as a power (bellows for the
  roaster? blows smells downwind = free street marketing when he hangs around?)
- a **talking bird that rides gusts** — aerial scout (spots busy corners → location-
  strategy hints; arrives "on the wind" with district rumors)
- a **flamboyant chef** — big-energy culinary rival or mentor

**Mechanics hooks (DMN-fit):** wind/smell = diegetic marketing (roast aroma drifting =
foot-traffic buff Gusto can amplify); long-winded customer = service-speed teaching beat;
bird scout = demand-forecast hint vendor (pairs with weekday demand factors shipped in
wave 4).

**Care-flags:**
- ⚠ **Trademark/likeness:** "flamboyant chef" named Gusto is adjacent to *Gusteau*
  (Ratatouille/Disney-Pixar). If the chef form is picked, the name or the chef framing
  must change — don't combine them. The windbag/bird forms are clear.
- Name adjacency in-house: ART_BIBLE already has mascot candidate **Gus** (old-timer
  market expert) and the rescue-arc features **Old Joe** — if Gusto is promoted,
  consider renaming Gus or merging them (one gruff old-timer is enough).
- Family-friendly: huff-and-puff comedy stays kind (no mocking of breathlessness/asthma).

**Status:** logged, unscheduled. Candidate promotion target: P2 NPC roster (GDD B3).
